Privacy Policy for Pusoan App

Last updated: October 31, 2024

Welcome to Pusoan App! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Application includes:

  • Personal Data: When you register using Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture URL as provided by Google. If you register manually, we may ask for your full name and mobile number. This is managed through Firebase Authentication.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your Watch History, messages in Pusoan Chat, comments on videos, and answers to surveys.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics to automatically collect information about your device and how you interact with our app (e.g., device type, IP address, features used).
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect data to provide personalized advertising.
  • Video Playback Data: Since we use an embedded YouTube player, Google (as the provider of YouTube) may collect data about the videos you watch within our app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide a personalized experience (e.g., showing your name and profile picture).
  • Enable app features like Watch History, Rewards, and Chat.
  • Analyze app usage to monitor performance and improve our services.
  • Display advertisements to support the app.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share information with third-party service providers that perform services for us or on our behalf, including:

  • Google: We share data with Google for services like Firebase (Authentication, Analytics, Firestore), AdMob (advertising), and YouTube (video playback).
  • Shopping Partners (Lazada/Shopee): If you click a link to shop, you will be redirected to their site or app. Their own privacy policy will apply once you leave our app.

4. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that no security measure is perfect or impenetrable.

5. Contact Us
